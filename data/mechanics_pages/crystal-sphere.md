The Crystal Sphere event isn't just a simple choice — it's a hidden-object game on an **11×11 grid**. You get 3 divinations (or 6 if you take on a Debt curse), each revealing cells using a "Big" (3×3) or "Small" (single cell) tool. The grid contains a relic, 3 potions, 3 card rewards of increasing rarity, gold piles (10g and 30g), and a hidden Doubt curse occupying a 2×2 area.
