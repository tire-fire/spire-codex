---
title: Campfire Options
description: All 9 rest site choices - Rest, Smith, Dig, Lift, Cook, Clone, Hatch, Mend, and Kindle. Some require specific relics or cards.
category: mechanics
order: 12
---

| Option | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Rest | Heal 30% of max HP |
| Smith | Upgrade 1 card |
| Dig | Obtain a random relic (requires Shovel) |
| Lift | Gain +1 Strength at the start of each combat per lift (requires Girya, max 3 lifts) |
| Cook | Remove 2 cards, gain 9 Max HP (requires Meat Cleaver) |
| Clone | Duplicate each card enchanted with Clone (requires Pael's Growth) |
| Hatch | Hatch Byrdonis Egg into Byrdpip pet (requires Byrdonis Egg) |
| Mend | Heal an ally for 30% of their max HP (multiplayer only) |
| Kindle | Relight the Pumpkin Candle (requires Pumpkin Candle) |
